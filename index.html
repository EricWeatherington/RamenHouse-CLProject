Ramen House


Our Shop

Established in 2018. We are bringing traditional Ramen to the City of Louisville! We also deliver Korean inspired rice bowls and we hand make our gyoza (dumplings). We don't try to do a lot of things half-decent, we try to do what we do very well. Come check us out!

Menu

Variety of Ramen options!
We serve Pork, Chicken, and Vegetarian broths. Our menu also includes Korean style Donburi rice bowls, delicious buns, and our very own handmande Gyoza (dumplings).
